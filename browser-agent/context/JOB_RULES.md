# Job Filtering Rules

## Include

- Role title contains: Full Stack, Software Engineer, Frontend,
  Backend.
- Experience range intersects with candidate profile.
- At least 2 required skills match candidate skill set.
- Location is in preferred list or marked remote.

## Exclude

- Internship/fresher-only jobs when candidate has mid-level
  experience.
- Irrelevant domains requiring specialized non-matching stack.
- Jobs with explicit low confidence signals (spam-like postings).
